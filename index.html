The Problem

Interviewed potential users have reported that it is challenging to find local businesses that sell high- quality clothing that is sustainable and fairly priced.

Others have found that it is tough to sustain their small business as they do not feel like they have the necessary tools and resources to focus on promotion.

Find the best local clothing brands and encourage sustainability in fashion!

Empathizing with Users

I interviewed 2 different types of potential users for this website: people who work in the fashion industry, and those who love fa- shion and want to purchase more locally and ethically.

Main Pain Points

1

Small clothing brands found in the past are very expensive.

2

Small clothing brands found in the past have lower quality clothing than big fast fashion companies.

3

Not having an effective tool to filter and locate great local brands.

User Flow

Wireframes

The low-fidelity wireframes were modified after conducting a usability study with 5 participants who are target users of this website.

Main Pain Points

1

The About Us page was hard to find for most users and the link did not work properly.

2

The “match me with a brand” flow did not have back and forth navigation.

3

The prototype had too much text and information most users do not read. These were eliminated to make the homepage simpler and include more images.

Accessibility Considerations

01.

The color palette was chosen in terms of the accessibility by basing myself of the contrast checker of webaim.org. The contrast ratio is quite good.

02.

The buttons and icons (calls to action) are quite big and adapted for bigger and smaller screens.

03.

The typography allows screen readers to understand the hierarchy of the website for visually impaired users.

Takeaways

Impact

The feedback of my project has been largely positive:
“This website is useful and will allow people to shop more locally and encourage sustainability as opposed to fast fashion.”

What I learned

A new project that allowed me to create a website to serve the community and give a potential solution to the fast fashion problem. This product is not for everyone, but having diverse opinions certainly helped my project to evolve in a better, more accessible direction.
